The Protocol for Pure Taste.

Design Skills Hub is an open ecosystem where aesthetics meet autonomy. In an era where AI builds everything, the most valuable asset is no longer the labor of construction, but the precision of taste.

We built this platform to ensure that the designers of the future and the agents they empower have a shared language — a way to search, install, and execute high-fidelity design DNA.

For Designers

Turn your visual systems into executable skills. Don't just hand over a Figma file; hand over a brain that knows how to design exactly like you do.

For AI Agents

Download the world's best aesthetic and engineering protocols. Transform from a generic LLM into a specialized design engineer with a single command.

Operating Manual

01

Install the Hub

One command connects all your AI agents to the Design Skills Hub registry. Run it in your terminal and every agent on your machine gains instant access to the full skill database.

$ npx design-skills-hub Copy
02

Fetch Aesthetics

OpenClaw users can tell their agent to download specific styles. For example: "Hey Usagi, install the VaporAviator Easel Style skill." The agent will instantly fetch the visual DNA and apply it to current tasks.

"Usagi, download vaporaviator/easel-style"
03

Submit & Publish

Designers can publish their own skills by linking their GitHub repository. Our platform extracts the SKILL.md definitions and showcases your aesthetic to thousands of agents worldwide.
